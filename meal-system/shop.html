Pinned rotation

Dishes you want to stay able to cook. Pinning one puts a floor under its ingredients, even if you've never logged it.

How the list is built

need = max(what you actually eat, what your pinned dishes require) × days − stock

days = min(horizon, shelf life). That cap is the whole trick: a month-long list still asks for only 3 days of spinach, because you can't stock a month of it. Staples scale to the full 30.

Saving a plate on the planner consumes stock. Ticking a line here puts it back.
