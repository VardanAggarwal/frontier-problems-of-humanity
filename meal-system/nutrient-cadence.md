# Nutrient Cadence — month / week / day / meal

_Feature 1 of the planner. What the body needs, bucketed by **how often** it must be satisfied. Derived from [meal-plan.md](meal-plan.md) §1 + profile. v1 · July 2026._

**Profile:** 68.5 kg · veg + egg · low-dairy · lightly active · goal fat ↓ / muscle ↑ · maintenance ~2,000–2,150 kcal → deficit target **1,700–1,850 kcal**.

The point of cadence: stop chasing everything on every plate. Some things are a once-a-month supplement, some rotate across a week, some are the daily contract, and only **protein** is genuinely a per-meal gate.

---

## Per meal
The only true per-plate gate. Everything else rolls up to the day.

| Requirement | Target / meal | Why per-meal |
|---|---|---|
| **Protein** | **~30 g** main meal · ~20 g snack | Muscle synthesis is better with an even 4-way split than 2 big hits; also the simplest single check per plate |
| Vit-C source | ≥1 at any iron meal | Doubles plant-iron uptake — must co-occur with the dal/greens, can't be averaged |
| A green | ≥1 at lunch **and** dinner | Volume + micro floor; placement matters, so it's a plate rule not a daily total |

## Per day
The daily contract. Hit these across the day's meals; the planner sums a plate's contribution toward them.

| Requirement | Target / day | Risk | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Protein (total) | 120 g | High | food + whey |
| Calories | 1,700–1,850 kcal | — | food |
| Carbs | 180–220 g | Low | food |
| Fat | ~50 g | Low | food |
| Fibre | 30–40 g (ramp) | Medium | legumes, veg, seeds |
| **Iron** | ~17 mg | High | horse gram/legumes/greens **+ sour** |
| **Calcium** | ~1,000 mg | High | Cipcal + ragi/til/tofu/curd |
| Vitamin C | ~85 mg | Low | amla/lemon/guava — also the iron partner |
| Folate | 400 µg | Low | legumes, greens |
| Omega-3 (ALA) | 1 tbsp ground flax | Medium | flax daily |
| Probiotic | 1 serving | High | curd / buttermilk / idli |
| B12 (supplement) | 2.4 µg | High | supplement — take daily |
| Calcium (supplement) | Cipcal | High | supplement — take daily |
| Iodine | 150 µg | Low | iodised salt (trivially met) |
| Water | 2.5–3 L | — | scale with fibre or it constipates |

## Per week
Rotate or average across the week — storable, or capped, or variety-driven. No need to hit daily.

| Requirement | Weekly handling |
|---|---|
| Vitamin A | Fat-soluble, stores — average across the week via carrot / sweet potato / moringa / papaya |
| Selenium | Covered by eggs eaten through the week |
| Zinc | 10–12 mg/day ideal but bank it weekly — pumpkin seeds, sprouted dals, til |
| Magnesium / Potassium | Broadly covered; treat as weekly, not a daily chase |
| Soya cap | **Max 2–3×/week** — a ceiling, not a target |
| Colour variety | Rotate plant colours across the week (microbiome diversity) |
| Resistance training | **2–3×/week** — without it this is lean-out only |

## Per month
Set-and-forget. Supplements and checks.

| Requirement | Monthly handling |
|---|---|
| **Vitamin D3** | Loading (60K schedule) then maintenance — dose by blood test |
| Blood test | Once up front — sets D3 dose, confirms B12/iron/Ca; then skip |
| Weigh-in | Weekly weigh-in vs the 0.3–0.5 kg/wk rule (weekly, but reviewed monthly) |
