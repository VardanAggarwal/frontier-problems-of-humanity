The daily contract

How this is built

Dish choice is weighted by what you already eat — dishes whose ingredients show up in your logged plates score higher, so the menu looks like your life, not a textbook.

The micro layer is not optional. Flax, seeds, moringa, a green and a sour are placed into slots as real items — never dropped as "garnish". They carry omega-3, calcium, zinc, magnesium and the iron-absorption partner.

Repairs can only add toppers, one serving per day. That is why a shortfall gets fixed with a spoon of pumpkin seeds and not four kilos of soya.
